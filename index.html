About Myself:

My name is Craig Webb. I'm a web developer and designer based in Burlington, VT. I've worked in the creative industry for 13 years, both as a contractor and agency employee. My first design job was at a small vinyl graphics shop in Downeast Maine. I spent the next several years working as a designer for an inbound marketing agency that focused on using HubSpot and WordPress as CMS platforms for clients. During my time with this agency, I designed numerous websites, landing pages, email templates and other advertising materials.


In 2014, I started to shift my focus to front-end web development after being provided the opportunity to develop the projects I had designed. I've continued to work as a developer since and have worked with several agencies on a wide range of project types.
